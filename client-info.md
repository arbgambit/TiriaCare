## About
- **Founder:** María Gilma Tiría
- **Credentials:** Medical Aesthetics & Holistic practitioner
- **Experience:** 45 years in Colombia and Canada
- **What does the business do?** TiriaCare is a proprietary system of vacuum cups (copas) invented by María Gilma Tiría. The cups are unique — nothing like them exists on the market. They use vacuum technology combined with non-invasive techniques to treat aesthetic conditions, prepare patients for surgery, accelerate post-surgical recovery, and restore skin damaged by previous procedures.
- **Mission/Vision:** Help people achieve safe, effective aesthetic results without pain, trauma, or invasive procedures. Also trains other aestheticians through courses and certifications.
- **Key Differentiators:**
  - Invented and developed by the founder herself over 6 years
  - No other product like it on the market
  - Pain-free, no redness, no discomfort
  - Results visible from the first session
  - Works for pre-op, post-op, AND standalone aesthetic treatments
  - Also effective as a gym complement for stubborn fat areas

## Target Audience
- **Primary audiences:**
  1. People preparing for cosmetic surgery (pre-operative)
  2. People recovering from cosmetic surgery (post-operative)
  3. People with bad surgery results needing restoration (fibrosis, uneven skin, numbness)
  4. Gym-goers who can't lose fat in stubborn areas (abdomen, back, legs, arms)
  5. People wanting non-surgical body contouring (cellulite, skin laxity, etc.)
  6. Aestheticians looking for training/certification in the method
- **Pain points the business solves:**
  - Stubborn fat that won't go away with exercise
  - Fibrosis, uneven skin, numbness after surgery
  - Fear of surgical complications
  - Need for proper surgical preparation
  - Slow post-op recovery

## Products — The Cups (Copas)
1. **Silhouette** — Breaks fat fibers and loosens fat. Used to prepare for liposuction or standalone fat reduction. Pain-free, no redness. 4-5 sessions with visible results from session one. Smaller size available for neck, arms, and face.
2. **LPG Endermology** — Designed for post-operative recovery. Creates drainage of fluids, waste, and residue through drainage channels. Also adheres/tightens skin without surgical lifting.
3. **Endermology Restauradora** — For patients who weren't properly prepared before surgery and now have complications (fibrosis, skin ondulations, discoloration, numbness). Works on dermis and epidermis to regenerate collagen and break down fibrotic tissue. For face and body.

## Services / Treatments

### Aesthetic Body Contouring (non-surgical)
Conditions treated:
- Celulitis (piel de naranja)
- Flacidez cutánea
- Estrías
- Grasa localizada
- Retención de líquidos
- Falta de tonicidad muscular
- Fibrosis

Body areas treated:
- Abdomen, cintura, espalda, hombros, brazos, caderas, glúteos, piernas

How it works: Vacuum technology stimulates circulation, promotes lymphatic drainage, and improves skin elasticity — reshaping the body naturally and progressively.

### Pre-Operative Treatment
Benefits:
1. Optimizes surgical time — improves tissue conditions for the surgeon
2. Reduces surgical risks and complications
3. Less trauma, better recovery — reduces inflammation, fibrosis, fluid buildup
4. Prevents skin irregularities — avoids fibrosis, ondulations, hardening, numbness, persistent redness

### Post-Operative Treatment
Benefits:
1. Accelerates recovery — stimulates circulation and lymphatic drainage
2. Prevents and treats fibrosis — softens hardened collagen fibers
3. Reduces bruising and edema — eliminates accumulated fluids
4. Improves silhouette — defines contours, corrects surface irregularities

How it works:
- Vacuum stimulation activates blood circulation and lymphatic drainage
- Tissue regeneration promotes the body's natural recovery
- Safe, controlled application adapts to surgically sensitive areas

### Gym / Fitness Complement
- Targets stubborn areas that don't respond to exercise alone
- Abdomen, back, legs, arms, stomach
- Breaks down fat so the body can evacuate it naturally

### Training & Certification
- Courses for aestheticians on pre-operative and post-operative treatments
- Hands-on training with TiriaCare cups
